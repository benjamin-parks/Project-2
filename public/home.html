Animal Pet Adoptions and Rescues

At Paws and Claws Adoption, we are proud to have facilitated the adoption and rescue of over 500 pets each year. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that each animal finds a loving, forever home. By choosing to adopt, you help us continue our mission of saving lives and making a difference in the lives of countless animals.

group

Community Outreach Programs

Our commitment to animal welfare extends beyond our shelter walls through our robust community outreach programs. We offer educational workshops, free or low-cost spay/neuter services, and pet care resources to help pet owners in our community. Join us in making a positive impact by participating in our programs and spreading awareness about responsible pet ownership.

event_available

Volunteer Opportunities

Make a difference in the lives of animals by joining our team of dedicated volunteers. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or more, your time and effort will be greatly valued. Volunteers help with everything from walking dogs and socializing cats to assisting with events and administrative tasks. Get involved and experience the joy of giving back to our furry friends.
